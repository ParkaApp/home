What does Parka do?


Parka tracks parking real-time by utilizing OpenCV, MongoDB, Swift, and Python. It analyzes video real-time and updates the databases to reflect the amount of cars in the parking garage.


  • Who are we?

    We're college students who know the struggles that parking presents on a growing campus.

  • Why Parka?

    Parka is a solution to the parking epidemic found across America at every University.

  • The Implementation

    Implementation of Parka on a campus is easy, and cost-efficient. The software takes minimum effort to up-keep and run.
